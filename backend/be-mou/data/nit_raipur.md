# **MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MoU)**

**BETWEEN**
**NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY RAIPUR**

**AND**

**VALLURUPALLI NAGESWARA RAO VIGNANA JYOTHI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY (VNR VJIET) – HYDERABAD**

This memorandum of understanding entered into on 24 December 2024 between National Institute of Technology Raipur - 492010, Chhattisgarh, an autonomous institute under Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India with its registered office at Raipur 492010, Chhattisgarh (hereafter referred to a NITRR) of the first part

---

**AND**

Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNRVJIET), Hyderabad Telangana 500090, was established in the year 1995 with the permission of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and the Government of Andhra Pradesh. VNR VJIET is an autonomous Institute under UGC conferred in 2012, and recognized under Section 2(f) and Section 12(B) of UGC Act, 1956 (hereafter referred to as VNRVJIET, Hyderabad) of the second part.

---

## **1.0 ABOUT THE INSTITUTES**

NITRR has been recognized as Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. Formerly NITRR was well known as Government Engineering College (GEC), Raipur first of its kind institutes established in 1956. In 2005, the College was renamed as National Institute of Technology Raipur and got the status of Institute of National Importance. NITRR offers graduate, post graduate and doctoral programmes in different disciplines, contributing to the nation by developing qualified human resources.

‘Vignana Jyothi’ is a non-profit organization founded by a group of Industrialists, Academicians, Professionals and non-resident Indians with an idea of imparting purposeful education to youth. Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNRVJIET), Hyderabad, Telangana sponsored by the Vignana Jyothi, was established in the year 1995 with the permission of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and the Government of Andhra Pradesh. The Institute offers 14 UG-B.Tech programmes and 13 PG-M.Tech Programmes and Ph.D Programmes.

---

## **2.0 PREAMBLE**

In order to facilitate collaborative research work, to establish academic and scientific relationships, to promote joint Research and Consultancy activities, to extend mutual support in Postgraduate and Doctoral programme, NITRR and VNR VJIET agree to establish this Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).

---

## **3.0 THE SCOPE OF MOU**

### **(A) ACADEMIC AND RESEARCH COLLABORATIONS IN AREAS OF MUTUAL INTEREST**

a) Faculty from NITRR and VNR VJIET-Hyderabad will engage in collaborative research work, support and help each other in joint research proposals, projects and consultancy work.

b) Joint project proposals can be developed by the faculty members from NITRR and VNR VJIET- Hyderabad to submit to funding agencies such as DST, DBT, UGC, AICTE, NRB, DRDO, etc.

c) Any intellectual property rights (IPR) in connection with the joint projects shall belong to both the institutions.

d) The visible output in the form of conference proceedings, journal publications and book chapters which resulted from the collaborative research work, joint projects and research work that leads to Ph.D. degree, shall belong to the faculty members and credentials shall be given to them who involved and executed the work as per the research and publishing ethics.

e) The research facilitates of NITRR can be utilized by the students and faculty of VNR VJIET- Hyderabad as per institute norms with applicable fee. Similarly, the research facilities at VNR VJIET campuses can be utilized by the students and faculty of NITRR as per the Institute norms.

f) VNRVJIET faculty members may be allowed to do the Ph.D programme on Full-time/Part-time basis as per institute norms of NITRR.

g) Both the institutions may jointly organize the workshops/Seminars/Faculty Development programmes in the areas of interest.

h) Faculty Summer Research Fellowship – Faculty of VNRVJIET may be allowed to work with the faculty members of NITRR as per the requirement of both parties.

i) Faculty of NITRR and VNRVJIET Hyderabad to develop joint societal impact projects and collaborations with other external agencies/ R&D organizations/ Indian and overseas Educational Institutions.

---

### **(B) EXCHANGE OF ACADEMIC AND SCIENTIFIC INFORMATION**

a) NITRR and VNR VJIET- Hyderabad can support and help each other in the academic programmes by conducting joint workshop, conferences, seminars, FDP by arranging short visits, guest lectures and training programmes for the faculty members and students.

b) Faculty members shall share articles, publications, scientific information, patents information etc. for their academic needs and not for commercial purposes on mutual consent and case to case basis with regard to revenue sharing and expenditure.

c) Students of VNR VJIET- Hyderabad campus may visit NITRR for 6 to 8 weeks to do summer internship work under the guidance of the NITRR faculty based on the availability. Students of NITRR can visit VNR VJIET- Hyderabad campuses for their internships / mini-projects/projects under the guidance of VNR VJIET- Hyderabad faculty.

d) Students of VNR VJIET- Hyderabad and NITRR may do 4th year project work based on the mutual interest under the joint guidance of the faculty members from VNR VJIET-Hyderabad and NITRR.

e) Faculty from NITRR may visit VNR VJIET- Hyderabad to teach a part of any course of B.Tech programme with prior permission from NITRR. Emoluments shall be paid to the faculty as per the VNR VJIET- Hyderabad norms.

f) Faculty from VNRVJIET- Hyderabad may be allowed to visit NITRR for 6 to 8 Weeks as summer research fellow to work on a project under the guidance of NITRR faculty.

---

## **4.0: MOU MODALITIES**

a) This MoU shall be effective immediately as soon agreed and signed by the both parties. This MoU shall be valid for a period of 3 years from the date of signing of the MoU.

b) Any party may terminate this MoU by giving a written notice of termination at least one months in advance.

c) In the event of termination of MoU, for whatever the reason, both parties agree to complete the committed joint projects, joint conferences, Ph.D. guidance etc. during the notice period and beyond.

---

## **5.0: MOU MODIFICATION OR AMENDMENT**

a) Any article or clause of this MoU can be removed or modified or any amendment can be included with the combined agreement from VNR VJIET- Hyderabad and NITRR.

b) This MoU can be reviewed and revised by both parties at any point of time if required on mutual agreement.

---

## **6.0: SEPARATE AGREEMENTS**

Each activity mentioned in this MoU is governed by terms and conditions agreed by both parties. If required, both parties can have an additional case specific agreement by negotiation and mutual agreement by signing a separate agreement or by amending a clause to the existing MoU.

---

## **7.0: ARBITRATION**

a) If any dispute arises from the agreement, the appropriate authorities from VNR VJIET-Hyderabad and NITRR can discuss and resolve the dispute in good faith.

b) If any dispute, issue or controversy arises from this agreement is not resolved with discussion and mutual understanding within 3 months, an arbitrator can be appointed by mutually consent to settle the issue as per the governing Indian laws.
